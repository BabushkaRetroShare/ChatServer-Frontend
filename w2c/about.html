This is a ChatServer for RetroShare
This is only provided for introduction and testing and bootstrapping of the RetroShare Network.
You can add your RetroShare Key and you will get the RS-Key from the ChatServer to add him to your friendlist
ChatServer has access to certain Chatrooms and will share them to you.
No File Transfer. No Forums. No Channels.
You need Friends in RetroShare to get access to stuff
Join a few chatrooms and start talking to others
This ChatServer has a maximum of 100 Friends.
You will get deleted from the ChatServer after another 100 Users add a RS-Key (FIFO)
Chatserver ONLY gives access to certain Chatrooms
